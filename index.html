Kazi Nazirul Islam

The national poet of bangladesh


Rebal poet

kazi nazerul Islam is the nation poet of bangladesh who is especially recognized as a Bidrohi Kobi(Rebal poet)around the country. Nazerul is a famous poet,musician and philosopher of bangladesh. The famous nazerul wirtting deel with the revolution with the period of the british governament. His revolutionary atempt helped him to be a national poet of bangladesh.He raised his voice aggainest any kind of fasisum and oppersiion and got popularity swiftly around the indian subcontinent.

Awesome writer

Nazerul's writting explored themes such as love,freedom,humanity and revolution.He oposed all forms of bigotry, fundamentalism, including religious cast based and gender based. Throught out his carrer, Nazerul wrote short stories, novels and esseys but is bust know for his songs and poems. He poinnered new music forms such as bengali ghazalas. Nazerul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramphone records),collectively know as Nazrul Geeti.
